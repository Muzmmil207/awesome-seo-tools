All in one SEO tools

From keyword research to link analysis, these tools are the Swiss army knives of the SEO world.

  • Ahrefs - Everything you need to rank higher & get more traffic.

  • SEMrush - Do SEO, content marketing, competitor research, PPC and social media marketing from just one platform.

  • Seodity - Seodity is a complete SEO toolkit to grow your business.

  • Ubersuggest - Want more traffic? Ubersuggest shows you how to win the game of SEO.

  • Mangools - Juicy SEO tools you will love.

  • SERanking - All-in-one agency-tailored SEO platform.

  • Moz Pro - SEO software and data to help you increase traffic, rankings, and visibility in search results.

  • Serpstat - SEO management platform.

  • SEO PowerSuite - Rankings, on-page, backlinks, and reports — all in one easy-to-use toolkit.

  • WebCEO - SEO & digital marketing tools. For agency-level analysis, reports & lead generation.

  • SpyFu - SEO marketing suite, PPC analyzer, historic data, competitive analysis, backlink outreach, unlimited keyword & domain projects, custom reporting.

  • Zutrix - SEO Tools to boost your traffic.

  • BrightEdge - BrightEdge is an enterprise SEO platform for data-driven content optimization and competitive analysis, ideal for large businesses.

  • CognitiveSEO- This tool provides comprehensive backlink analysis and content auditing to enhance your SEO strategy.

  • Keyword Insights - Make Better Content Faster. Much Faster. Transform Your Content and SEO Strategy with AI.

Keyword Research

Uncover high-potential keywords to target and captivate your audience.

  • Google Keyword Planner - Free tool from Google Ads that provides keyword ideas and traffic estimates.

  • Keyword Tool - Find great keywords using Google Autocomplete.

  • KeySearch LSI Keyword - Enter any keyword to generate semantically related LSI keywords to improve your SEO.

  • WordStream - One tool to discover and prioritize the best keywords to target.

  • Ahrefs’ Keywords Generator - Free keyword generator ideas by Ahref.

  • Semrush’s Keyword Magic Tool - Find millions of keyword suggestions for your SEO.

  • Long Tail Pro - The best keyword research tool for long tail keywords.

Backlink Analysis

Delve deep into your link profile and discover opportunities for growth and risk mitigation.

  • Majestic - Find out who links to your website

  • Monitor Backlinks - Check the good and bad backlinks for you and the competition.

  • Respona - The all in one link building tool

  • Other “All in one SEO tools” offer this feature as well.

Content Optimization

Fine-tune your content to resonate with search algorithms and your audience alike.

  • Clearscope - Clearscope is the best-in-class SEO content optimization platform that drives search traffic.

  • SurferSEO - Don’t leave your SEO strategy up to chance. Surfer will give you a SEO workflow to boost your organic traffic, increase your visibility, and improve your rank.

  • NeuronWriter - Optimize your website content so Google adores it.

  • ContentSwift - An open source content research/optimization tool for SEO.

  • Market Muse - Our AI analyzes your entire website to give you personalized, objective insights so you can confidently plan better and faster.

  • Frase io - Frase is the fastest and easiest way to create content that ranks on Google.

  • GrowthBar - Write and optimize SEO-friendly blog content. 10x faster with GrowthBar’s AI.

  • LSIGraph - Write SEO content with confidence and 10X Your site’s traffic.

  • Yoast SEO - WordPress plugin for SEO optimization of posts and pages.

Rank Tracking

Monitor your website’s position in search results and gauge your SEO strategies’ effectiveness.

  • SerpBear - An open source search engine position tracking app.

  • SERPWatcher - The most advanced rank tracker for marketers and agencies.

  • AccuRanker - The world’s fastest & most accurate rank tracker.

  • Nightwatch - Nightwatch is a cloud-based SEO and analytics tool that focuses on rank tracking and reporting.

  • Other “All in one SEO tools” offer this feature as well.

Technical SEO

Ensure your website’s foundation is solid for search engines and user experience.

  • Google Search Console - Free tool from Google to monitor and maintain site’s presence in Google Search results.

  • Screaming Frog SEO Spider - SEO Spider is a website crawler that helps you improve onsite SEO by auditing for common SEO issues.

  • SiteAnalyzer - Scan your websites and get detailed information about each page.

  • Screpy - Stop worrying about missing important website metrics or checking multiple platforms!

  • Sitebulb - The website auditing tool for SEO consultants and agencies.

  • Moz On-Page Grader - Analyzes individual page optimization.

  • BROWSEO - See your site through the eyes of a search engine. BROWSEO gives you the type of x-ray vision that search engines have.

Local SEO

Boost your local presence and connect with audiences in your community.

  • BrightLocal -The complete platform to monitor, audit, and improve local SEO.

  • Whitespark - Everything you need for better local SEO.

  • LocalFalcon - The most accurate local rank tracker.

  • Grid My Business - The quickest way to discover your local visibility.

  • Moz Local - Maximize your online visibility with Moz’s powerful local SEO and reputation management tool.

SEO Browser Extensions

Equip your browser with tools for quick and efficient SEO insights on the go.

  • SEO Minion - SEO Minion helps you in your daily SEO tasks such as On-Page SEO analysis, Broken Link Checking, SERP Preview and more.

  • SEOquake - A powerful SEO toolbox for your browser.

  • Woorank - Run your Review today to get an instant website analysis and learn what you need to do to improve.

  • Keyword Surfer - Chrome Extension for content and keyword research.

  • MozBar - Provides instant metrics for any page or SERP.

  • Ahrefs’ SEO Toolbar - On-page SEO report, broken link checker, redirect tracer, and country changer for search results in one extension.

  • Redirect Path - The HTTP header & redirect checker for SEO.

  • META SEO inspector - Easily inspect the meta data found inside web pages, find issues and get advice to fix them.

  • Similarweb - Instant website analysis, including site rank, site traffic, sources and geography.

  • MST SERP Counter - SERP Counter gives accurate search results of a searched keyword. We can see the position, result, and rank.

  • TextOptimizer - It analyzes search results for relevant terms and extracts “intent tables” to suggest other words that you can use in your copy “to better match search engines’ expectations.”

  • SEO Search Simulator - With this extension, you can emulate Google searches from any location and check if a particular URL ranks in the top 100 results.

  • BuzzSumo - Browser Extension that packs a punch when it comes to content research, link analysis, and social media investigation.

  • Google Lighthouse - Browser Extension that offers detailed reports and actionable tips for enhancing your site’s SEO performance such as Page load speeds, Accessibility, SEO and more.

Validator / Checker

  • Broken Link Checker - Detects and reports broken links on websites.

  • Robots.txt Checker - Checks robots.txt files for errors.

  • Lighthouse Rich Result Checker - Tests whether your website is eligible for rich results in Google Search.

  • XML Sitemap Checker - Validates XML sitemaps for errors.

Miscellaneous Tools

Explore a diverse range of tools for those niche tasks and unique SEO challenges.

  • GTmetrix - Analyzes site speed and provides performance insights.

  • Pingdom - Tests website load time and gives performance analysis.
